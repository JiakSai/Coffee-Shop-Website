This policy applies to all users accessing our website and may be modified from time to time. Tracking Technologies

When you visit our website, "cookies," which are small data files stored on your computer, are used. Cookies allow you to shop on our website and can be used to improve or personalise your online browsing, shopping, and email communication experience. We also use cookies to determine which of our advertisements are displayed to you on other websites.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or to notify you when one is sent, giving you the option of accepting or rejecting it. However, if you refuse to accept cookies, you will be unable to access your account information or make purchases on our websites..

How do we manage cookies?

A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier amongst other, that is sent and stored on your computer or mobile device in emails and or notifications that we send to you on our Website and or App. Cookies are required to use our Website and or App. Third parties may be able to associate the Personal Information they collect with other personal information they have about you from other sources. We do not necessarily have access to or control over the cookies they use. You have the right to choose to disable, block or deactivate cookies. Please note that however refusal or removal of some cookies could affect the availability, functionality or use of our Website and our App.
